Common Questions

Before you reach out

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on scope. A single vanity typically takes 2–4 weeks from deposit to install. A full kitchen can take 6–12 weeks. We provide an accurate timeline with every proposal.

Do you handle design, or just building?

Both. We handle the full process from initial design drawings through final installation. We can also work from your architect's or designer's plans if you already have them.

What wood species and finishes do you offer?

We work in virtually any hardwood — oak, maple, cherry, walnut, hickory, poplar, and more. For finishes, we offer painted, stained, or natural looks in any sheen level. Bring a sample, a photo, or just a feeling and we'll match it.

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due on completion. For larger projects we may structure milestone payments — your proposal will detail the schedule clearly.
