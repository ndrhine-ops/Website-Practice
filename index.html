Our Story

Where tradition
meets modern craft

Rhinehart Cabinetry was built on a simple belief: that quality woodworking should be accessible without sacrificing artistry. We combine time-honored joinery with contemporary design sensibility.

Every piece that leaves our shop is built to live in your home for generations — not years. We stand behind our work from the first consultation to the final coat of finish.
