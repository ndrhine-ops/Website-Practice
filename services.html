What We Do

Our services

Commercial cabinetry for restaurants, offices, and retail — and residential projects built to the same standard. Every job gets our full craft.

In Detail

What each cabinet type includes

01

Base Cabinets

The foundational component of any commercial build. We fabricate base cabinets to handle demanding environments — kitchens, workrooms, labs, and service areas.

  • Workroom and laboratory base cabinets
  • Commercial kitchen base runs
  • Sink and utility cabinet configurations
  • Corner and specialty base options
  • Frameless and face-frame construction
02

Wall Cabinets

High-quality wall-mount cabinetry built for storage-intensive commercial spaces — designed for high-traffic environments where durability and accessibility matter.

  • General commercial storage cabinets
  • Corner wall cabinet solutions
  • Appliance and equipment wall cabinets
  • Custom sizing to fit any wall layout
  • Heavy-duty material construction
03

Tall Cabinets

Versatile floor-to-ceiling storage built to maximize capacity in any commercial setting — tool and supply storage, pantries, or high-density utility configurations.

  • Broom, pantry, and utility tall cabinets
  • High-density storage configurations
  • Tool and supply storage options
  • Adjustable interior shelving
  • ADA-compliant configurations available
04

Hutch Cabinets

Pre-designed upper storage systems built to pair with base configurations — adding organized overhead storage to any commercial workstation or counter run.

  • General overhead storage hutches
  • Corner hutch configurations
  • Open and closed door options
  • Designed to coordinate with base runs
  • Custom depth and width available
05

Custom Casework

When standard sizes won't cut it, we build to your exact specifications — commercial casework designed around your floor plan, your workflow, and your environment.

  • Restaurant and hospitality millwork
  • Reception desks and lobby casework
  • Bar fronts and back-bar cabinetry
  • Retail display cases and shelving
  • Lab and specialty-grade casework
06

Countertops

Custom countertops fabricated to integrate seamlessly with your commercial cabinetry — built for the demands of your environment and finished to your specifications.

  • Commercial kitchen and bar countertops
  • Restaurant and hospitality surfaces
  • Workroom and laboratory tops
  • Integrated and undermount sink cutouts
  • Custom edge profiles and color matching
