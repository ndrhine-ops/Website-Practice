In Detail

What each service includes

01

Kitchen Cabinetry

The heart of your home deserves the best. We design and build kitchen cabinetry that balances beauty, function, and durability.

  • Base, wall, and tall cabinet runs
  • Frameless and face-frame construction
  • Shaker, flat-panel, raised-panel, and custom door styles
  • Pull-out shelves, lazy susans, and drawer organizers
  • Island and peninsula cabinetry
02

Bathroom Vanities

Custom bath cabinetry built to handle the demands of daily use while looking impeccable for years to come.

  • Single and double vanity configurations
  • Floating and floor-mount designs
  • Medicine cabinets and linen towers
  • Built-in hampers and specialty storage
  • Moisture-resistant construction and finishes
03

Built-In Storage

Make every inch work harder. Our built-ins are designed around your life — not a catalog layout.

  • Bookcases and library walls
  • Entertainment centers with cable management
  • Mudroom cubbies and bench seats
  • Closet systems and wardrobe built-ins
  • Window seats with hidden storage
04

Custom Furniture

When you can't find exactly what you're looking for, we build it. Every piece is made to measure and made to impress.

  • Dining tables and benches
  • Desks and credenzas
  • Bed frames and headboards
  • Coffee tables and side tables
  • One-of-a-kind statement pieces
05

Home Office

Purpose-built cabinetry for the modern home office — designed for productivity and built to look exceptional on any video call.

  • Built-in desk systems and workstations
  • Floor-to-ceiling bookcase walls
  • Filing and document storage
  • Cable management integration
  • Convertible and Murphy desk options
06

Finishing & Refinishing

A great finish is what separates good cabinetry from great cabinetry. We take our finishing as seriously as our joinery.

  • Spray lacquer in any sheen level
  • Stain matching and custom color formulation
  • Paint and primed finishes
  • Glazing and antiquing effects
  • Cabinet door and drawer face refinishing
07

Solid Surface Work

Professional solid surface fabrication and installation — seamlessly integrated with your cabinetry for a cohesive, polished result.

  • Kitchen and bathroom countertops
  • Integrated and undermount sink cutouts
  • Vanity tops and backsplashes
  • Windowsill caps and surface thresholds
  • Custom color and edge profile matching
