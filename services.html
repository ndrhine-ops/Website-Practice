In Detail

What we do

01

Commercial

Full-scale commercial builds for restaurants, offices, retail spaces, and more. We take on projects of any size and deliver the same precision and craftsmanship every time.

  • Restaurant and hospitality interiors
  • Office and corporate environments
  • Retail and display buildouts
  • Medical and laboratory spaces
  • Large-scale and multi-unit projects
02

Cabinets

Custom cabinet fabrication built to your exact specifications — from a single vanity to a full commercial run. Face-frame or frameless, any wood species, any finish.

  • Base, wall, tall, and hutch cabinets
  • Face-frame and frameless construction
  • Kitchen, bathroom, and workroom cabinetry
  • Commercial and high-traffic configurations
  • Custom sizing and interior layouts
03

Woodworking

Precision woodworking for built-ins, casework, countertops, and specialty pieces — all handcrafted in our shop from materials selected for durability and beauty.

  • Built-in shelving and storage units
  • Reception desks and lobby casework
  • Bar fronts and back-bar builds
  • Countertops and solid surface work
  • Custom millwork and specialty pieces
04

Trim & Moulding

Custom trim and moulding that ties a space together — from clean modern profiles to detailed traditional millwork, installed with the same care as everything we build.

  • Crown and base moulding
  • Door and window casing
  • Chair rail and wainscoting
  • Custom profile moulding
  • Interior architectural detail work
