About Us

Built on tradition.

Decades of woodworking craft, carried forward with modern precision and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Story

Where every joint
tells a story

"We don't just build cabinets — we build the spaces where life happens."

Rhinehart Cabinetry began with a simple conviction: that exceptional woodworking shouldn't be reserved for a privileged few. Founded on decades of hands-on experience, we've grown from a small workshop into a trusted name in custom cabinetry — without ever losing sight of what matters most: the craft itself.

We work directly with homeowners, interior designers, and contractors to bring visions to life. Every project starts with listening, and ends with work that exceeds expectations.

When you work with Rhinehart, you're not just getting a cabinet company — you're getting a partner who cares as much about your space as you do.

What We Stand For

Our core values

Craftsmanship

We hold ourselves to the highest standard of woodworking, from the species of wood we select to the final coat of finish. Quality is not a feature — it's the foundation.

Integrity

Honest timelines and no hidden costs. We mean what we say and stand behind every piece we make — no exceptions, no excuses.

Longevity

We build for generations. The materials we choose, the joints we cut, the finishes we apply — all are selected for durability that outlasts trends and withstands decades of daily use.

Our History

A few milestones

1996

Founded. Rhinehart company starts as a team of carpenters looking to expand their craft and grow into their own company.

1998

Growth. Word of mouth drives our growth. We expand the shop and add our section for cabinets.

2003

Expansion. New work into the Arkansas Tri-State area accelerates and leads to an unprecedented amount of jobs.

2008

Cross-Country. Word of mouth and a grand portfolio helps to expand our work across the nation.

2014

More Work = More Room. Need for a larger shop and machines to maintain efficiency is required to keep true to our values.

2019

Second Location. We open our second location in Northwest Arkansas.

Today

Still Building. Same commitment, refined craft. We continue to take on projects large and small. Always with the same care that started it all in 1996.
